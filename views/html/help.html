How msgboy works

The msgboy figures out what you want to see on the web and presents it to you all in one place.

It will show you desktop notifications that tell when msgboy thinks a relevant story has been added to your dashboard and you can vote them up or down, which the msgboy uses to learn which things you care about the most.

Visit your msgboy dashboard to see all the stories that the msgboy gathered for you.

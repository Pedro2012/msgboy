Follow your web with msgboy

The msgboy uses your browsing and bookmarks to serve you a live stream of what you’re most interested in.

He’s smart, too! Plus or minus stories to help him know what you want to see more—or less—of.

Notifications

The msgboy uses Chrome desktop notifications to feed you what it thinks you’ll be most interested in.

You can hit the plus or minus to provide feedback right from the notification, or just click the story to visit it.

To adjust the frequency of notifications, visit the Settings by clicking the gear icon.

Your data is privately yours

The msgboy uses HTML5’s ability to store and access your data locally, so msgboy accesses it from your machine for your use, which makes it extra fast.

Your msgboy is yours — he doesn’t report home. Ever.

Smart + Customizable

Msgboy is a Google Chrome™ application that builds a custom feed of stories for you based on the websites you visit most and the ones you’ve bookmarked, and delivers them in realtime.

Msgboy also gives you the contols allowing you customize your feed so that you are seeing more of the stories you want and less of the ones that you don’t.

Private + Secure

We take your privacy extremely seriously and we do not store any information about msgboy’s users. Msgboy runs locally.

Your data stays with you, on your computer and is never sent to any server.

Open Source

We are strong believers in the open source movement, and Msgboy is open source. You can help make it better by improving its code, by reporting errors and by asking questions.

Available in the chrome web store

A quick note

When you install Msgboy, Google Chrome™ will ask if Msgboy can access some of your user data.

Msgboy stores your data locally, on your own machine. So while the app uses this information, it never sends it to anyone else.

Rest easy. Your information is secure.

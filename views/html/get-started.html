Subscribe to more sites

When you browse the web and your favorite sites, you may always decide to follow the page that you're on, so that when new content is added, your instantly notified. We will show a little bookmark on pages that will help you do that.

Receive notifications

Every time the msgboy learns about something new being published that you care about, it will show you an alert. Click it and you're sent to the right page!

Check your Stream

If you missed notifications, or if you've left your browser for a little while, don't you worry, we have you covered with an inbox that will help you see all the messages you've received at once, read them while you're offline, or even sort them.
